Offcanvas components

offcanvas 是從畫面側邊出現的元件，透過 JS 來切換期顯示與否

  • 外層包含 offcanvas, offcanvas-start，start, end, top, bottom 決定顯示方向
  • 必須給予一個特定 id
  • 內層包含 offcanvas-header, offcanvas-body, offcanvas-title 等樣式
